Here is a list of things managers need to do to maintain their Rakontu community.

These things should be done before any members are invited.

  • Decide on community settings like what you want people to read when they join the community and what sorts of things they can do in it.
  • Make sure you choose some nudge category names and questions. These are the rating scales along which stories and other entries will be "nudged" or rated by your community's members.
  • Decide what questions you want to ask about each type of entry.
  • Decide if you want to create any fictional characters that people can use to tell stories (somewhat) anonymously.
  • Add some system resources to help people get started. (You can also customize these to your particular needs.)
  • Invite some people to join your community, then start things off with some invitations to tell stories!

These things can be done after some members join.

  • Ask some people to become curators and guides. If you will have some off-line members of the community (perhaps older folks), ask some people to become liaisons. If you can't find people who want to take on these roles, do them yourself.
  • It's a good idea to have at least two managers, so you can share the tasks.
